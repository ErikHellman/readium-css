OpenType Features

Small Capitals

If the first line of this paragraph is using proper small capitals and not fake ones, which should be pretty obvious at first sight, then the font you’re testing support the OpenType Feature for small capitals.

Numerals

The following are lining-numerals:
9 8 7 6 5 4 3 2 1 0.

If those ones look the same i.e. they’re all aligned on the baseline, then the font doesn’t ship with oldstyle numerals:
9 8 7 6 5 4 3 2 1 0.
